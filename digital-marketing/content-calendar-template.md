# Content Calendar Template (Phase 2+)

Use **after** Google reviews baseline (15+) is established. Until then, energy goes to review asks and GBP posts — not daily Reels.

**Time budget:** 2–3 hours/week  
**Channels:** GBP posts (weekly) · Instagram (2 posts + 1 Reel/week) · YouTube (2 videos/month in Phase 2)

---

## Monthly themes (dermatology)

| Month | Theme | Example topics |
|-------|-------|----------------|
| Jan–Feb | Winter dry skin | Moisturizer routine, lip care |
| Mar–Apr | Wedding / event prep | Pre-bridal skin, laser timing |
| May–Jun | Summer / sun | SPF, tan removal, pigmentation |
| Jul–Sep | Monsoon | Fungal infections, acne flare |
| Oct–Nov | Festive glow | HydraFacial, party-ready skin |
| Dec | Hair fall season | PRP, hair transplant consult |

---

## Weekly template

| Day | Channel | Content type | CTA |
|-----|---------|--------------|-----|
| Mon | GBP | Tip post (text + 1 photo) | Call / Book |
| Wed | Instagram | Carousel or Reel (60 sec) | WhatsApp link in bio |
| Fri | Instagram | Before/after or machine (consent!) | DM / WhatsApp |
| Sat | YouTube (Phase 2) | 3–5 min explainer | Link in description |

---

## Content pillars (rotate)

1. **Condition education** — acne, melasma, hair fall (doctor on camera)
2. **Treatment walkthrough** — what happens in HydraFacial / laser session
3. **Myth busting** — “Is laser safe for Indian skin?”
4. **Machine trust** — Soprano Platinum, Tri-Beam (show device, not stock)
5. **Patient stories** — text quote from Google review (with permission), no face without consent
6. **Behind the clinic** — team, hygiene, consultation process

## Production checklist per piece

- [ ] Patient consent if any identifiable image
- [ ] Hindi caption optional (NCR audience)
- [ ] CTA: WhatsApp or Maps, not generic “link in bio” only
- [ ] Cross-post key Reels as YouTube Shorts
- [ ] UTM or ask “how did you find us?” to measure

---

## Paid boost (Phase 2 only)

- **Budget starter:** ₹10,000–15,000/month Meta
- **Target:** Women 25–45, 15 km radius Sector 85
- **Creative:** Best-performing organic Reel
- **CTA:** WhatsApp button
- **Kill rule:** Stop if cost per WhatsApp chat &gt; ₹500 after 2 weeks with no bookings
