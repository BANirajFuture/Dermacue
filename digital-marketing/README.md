# Dermacue — Digital Marketing

**Clinic:** Dermacue Skin, Laser and Hair Transplant Clinic · Sector-85, Faridabad  
**Primary acquisition channel:** Google Maps / Google Business Profile + reviews (local search)

## Why this folder exists

Phase 1 built the website and WhatsApp funnel. **Patients in Faridabad NCR discover clinics on Google first** — “dermatologist near me”, Maps, and star ratings — long before they visit Instagram or click ads.

Marketing here is ordered by **ROI for a new local clinic**, not by what looks flashy.

## Channel priority (Dermacue)

```
1. Google Business Profile + reviews     ← highest intent, lowest cost
2. Website (phase-1-website)             ← trust after they find you on Maps
3. WhatsApp                              ← booking & conversation (already wired)
4. Instagram                             ← awareness, before/after (Phase 1 light)
5. Practo / Justdial                     ← secondary discovery in NCR
6. Paid ads (Meta / Google)              ← Phase 2, after 15+ reviews
```
