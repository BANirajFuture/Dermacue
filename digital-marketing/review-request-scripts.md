# Review Request Scripts

Replace `REVIEW_LINK` with your short link from Google Business Profile → **Get more reviews**.

---

## In-clinic (reception / doctor)

**English (30 seconds):**

> If you're happy with today's visit, would you mind leaving us a quick Google review? It really helps other patients in Faridabad find us. You can scan this QR code — it takes less than a minute. Thank you.

**Hindi:**

> अगर आज का विज़िट आपको अच्छा लगा, तो क्या आप हमें Google पर एक छोटा सा review दे सकते हैं? इससे Faridabad के दूसरे मरीज़ों को हमें ढूंढने में मदद मिलती है। QR code स्कैन करें — एक मिनट से कम लगेगा। धन्यवाद।

## After hair transplant / multi-visit treatments

Ask for review **after a visible milestone** (e.g. 2-week follow-up with good progress), not on day 1 post-op when discomfort is normal.

```
Hi [Name], great to see your progress at today's follow-up.

If you're comfortable sharing your experience, a Google review would mean a lot to us:

REVIEW_LINK
```

---

## Notes

- Personalize `[Name]` and `[treatment]`
- Send during business hours (10 am – 5 pm), not late night
- Max 2 review asks per patient — in clinic + one WhatsApp
- Never condition discounts on reviews
